The Sunflower

The genus Helianthus L. includes about 70 species and about twenty accepted infra-specific taxa, of the almost 450 described,in the Compositae family (Asteraceae), practically all of which are native to North and Central America from where 52 of the valid species have been described;a few would be native to South Russia and Western Australia.
In the rest of the world the present species have all been introduced.
